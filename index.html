Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving growth with technology and creativity for a smarter, modern nation.

Sustainability

Protecting resources with eco-friendly initiatives and renewable energy solutions.

Infrastructure

Enhancing connectivity through modern transportation and urban development.

Empowerment

Building equity with education, skills, and inclusive opportunities for all.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions an inclusive, thriving nation driven by innovation, sustainability, and growth. By embracing new technologies, empowering individuals, and addressing challenges, we are creating a future where prosperity and opportunity are accessible to all, fostering progress and development for everyone.

Digital Transformation

Creating a digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"We must move beyond a world where profit is the only measure of success. In Bangladesh 2.0, business is not about maximizing wealth for a few; it is about solving social problems, addressing environmental challenges, and ensuring that the benefits of progress are shared by all. Our young generation, armed with knowledge and purpose, is the key. If we empower them to think differently, to create not just for profit but for the betterment of society, we will see a nation that thrives in every way economically, socially, and culturally."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
